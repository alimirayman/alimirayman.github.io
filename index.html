02 Ventures

What I'm building

Products solving real problems in healthcare, finance, and architecture

2021 — Present

Shastho Limited

Bangladesh’s healthcare system faces deep structural and accessibility challenges — nearly 65% of people with medical needs never reach formal healthcare facilities, let alone digital platforms. Shastho Limited was founded to build the nation’s most comprehensive digital healthcare infrastructure — connecting people, providers, and partners through seamless technology.

  • Unified Ecosystem: Digitizing healthcare across AI, diagnostics, telemedicine, pharmacy, and corporate wellness.
  • Sub-brands: Shastho AI (health intelligence), Shastho Card (membership & savings), Shastho Elite (concierge care), Shastho Corporate (employee wellness).
  • Infrastructure: Built on AWS, Node.js, and a scalable microservice architecture with real-time cloud orchestration.
  • Network: Collaborating with leading hospital groups, national diagnostics, and major corporate organizations across Bangladesh.
  • Vision: Making healthcare accessible, intelligent, and convenient for every household.
In Development

BusinessOS

Finance is one of the hardest and most expensive aspects of running a business in Bangladesh. To solve this, I'm building BusinessOS — a local-first ERP and desktop SaaS that makes business finance easy and affordable for entrepreneurs and SMEs. Built on an offline-first stack (Electron + Nuxt + SQLite) with event-sourced data models, allowing small businesses to operate reliably without needing constant connectivity.

Co-lead

ASSENT Technology

Luxury real estate and architecture practice. Through ASSENT, I collaborate with my wife, Architect Nazia Shafinaz, to create sustainable, well-designed, and affordable real estate projects — merging sustainability, aesthetics, and affordability to craft spaces that are practical yet beautiful.
